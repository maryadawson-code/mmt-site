Podcast Guest?

Fed UP is looking for guests with something real to say about federal health IT.

Pitch a Topic

What I'm Looking For

Story tips. Something doesn't add up? A program that's actually working? Leadership changes that matter? I want to know.

Hard questions. What's confusing about federal health IT? What analysis would actually help? The best content comes from real questions.

Different perspectives. Contractors, government leaders, clinicians, beneficiaries—if you have a view on what's working (or not) in military medicine, let's talk.

What I'm Not Looking For

Vendor pitches disguised as thought leadership. Sponsored content that doesn't serve readers. Pay-for-play arrangements.

If your main goal is getting your company mentioned, we're probably not a fit. If your goal is genuinely helping people understand this space better—even if that happens to involve your work—that's different.

The test is simple: Does it help readers understand what's actually happening? Does it serve the warfighter or the beneficiary?

About Fed UP Guest Appearances

Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality is a podcast I co-host with Sara Byrd. We bring different perspectives—I focus on strategic vision, policy, and enabling technology; Sara brings deep expertise on enterprise modernization and execution.

We're looking for guests who can speak candidly about what's actually happening. Government leaders, industry innovators, practitioners who have something real to say—not talking points.

The "No Infomercial" rule applies: Appearances are for substantive discussion. If the goal is a product pitch, we'll cut it.
