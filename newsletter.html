From Mary

I started this newsletter because I was tired of seeing the same patterns repeat. Vendors pitching innovation theater. Contractors missing the signals. Good technology dying in the valley of death while the warfighter waited.

Mission Meets Tech is my attempt to change that. Every issue is built around one question: Does this save lives or enhance readiness?

If you're a contractor trying to navigate DHA, a government leader making technology decisions, or anyone who cares about the mission, this newsletter is for you.

No talking points. No vendor spin. Just the unfiltered truth about what's happening in federal health IT.
