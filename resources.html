The Three Magic Words

What the Triad actually needs from your solution:

Governance

Bass needs AI that audits itself for compliance. If you can't demonstrate how your data flows into MHS GENESIS with full audit traceability, you're not ready for DHA.

Speed

Michael needs solutions that deploy into GenAI.mil and AskSage ecosystems by April. "Speed to capability delivery" is now the organizing principle of the Department of War.

Survivability

Via needs systems that work when the network goes dark. If your platform requires persistent connectivity to function, you're building for the wrong war.
