Key Terms & Acronyms

Common terminology in federal health IT and defense contracting.

DHA
Defense Health Agency – The combat support agency that manages the Military Health System
MHS
Military Health System – The enterprise that provides healthcare to service members, retirees, and dependents
MHS GENESIS
The DoD's new electronic health record system, built on Oracle Cerner
MTF
Military Treatment Facility – Hospitals and clinics operated by the military
TRICARE
The health care program for uniformed service members, retirees, and their families
NDAA
National Defense Authorization Act – Annual legislation authorizing DoD programs and spending
SBIR/STTR
Small Business Innovation Research / Small Business Technology Transfer – Federal R&D programs for small businesses
OTA
Other Transaction Authority – Alternative contracting mechanism for R&D and prototypes
ATO
Authority to Operate – Security authorization required to operate IT systems
FedRAMP
Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program – Cloud security authorization
H2F
Holistic Health and Fitness – Army's Human Performance Optimization program
HPO
Human Performance Optimization – Doctrine focused on warfighter readiness through health
RPM
Remote Patient Monitoring – Technology for tracking patient health outside clinical settings
DHMSM
Defense Healthcare Management Systems Modernization – The program office managing MHS GENESIS
8(a)
SBA program providing business development assistance to disadvantaged small businesses
IDIQ
Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity – Contract type with flexible ordering
