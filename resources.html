The Three Magic Words for Contractors

If you're pitching to the MHS Triad, your solution needs to address:

Governance

Bass needs AI that audits itself for compliance. Can your data be traced? Does it integrate with MHS GENESIS?

Speed

Michael needs solutions that deploy into GenAI.mil and AskSage ecosystems by April. Can you move fast?

Survivability

Via needs systems that work when the network goes dark. Does your platform function offline?
