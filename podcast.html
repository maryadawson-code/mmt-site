About the Show

Why "Fed UP"? Because we're fed up with the talking points. Fed up with vendors who pitch innovation theater while the warfighter waits. Fed up with policies that look great in PowerPoint but die in implementation.

But we're also fired up. Fired up about the leaders who get it, the technologies that actually work, and the mission that keeps us coming back to this space.

Each episode, we dive deep into the policy fights, technology bets, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT and defense innovation. No talking points. No vendor spin. Just unfiltered analysis from two practitioners who live this work every day.
