About the Show

Why "Fed UP"? Because we're fed up with the talking points. Fed up with vendors who pitch innovation theater while the warfighter waits. Fed up with policies that look great in PowerPoint but die in implementation. And frankly, fed up with watching the same patterns repeat while the threat environment accelerates.

But we're also fired up. Fired up about the leaders who get it, the technologies that actually work, and the mission that keeps us coming back to this space.
