About the Show

Why "Fed UP"? Because we're fed up with the talking points. Fed up with vendors who pitch innovation theater while the warfighter waits. And frankly, fed up with watching the same patterns repeat while the threat environment accelerates.

But we're also fired up. Fired up about the leaders who get it, the technologies that actually work, and the mission that keeps us coming back to this space.

Each episode, we dive deep into the policy fights, technology bets, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT and defense innovation. No scripts, no sponsor reads—just two practitioners telling you what's really happening.

Your Hosts

Two perspectives. One mission.

Mary Womack

Founder, Mission Meets Tech

Mary bridges health strategy and government/digital health, providing mission-focused intelligence through her newsletter reaching 1,100+ senior decision-makers. With deep expertise in defense health acquisition and data governance, she translates policy complexity into actionable insights.

Sara Byrd

Co-Host, Fed UP

Sara brings a complementary perspective to the federal health IT conversation. Together with Mary, she co-founded Fed UP to create the honest, practitioner-level discussions that the defense health community needs but rarely gets.

Fed UP is a 50/50 partnership between Mary and Sara.

What We Cover

Leadership & Strategy

Technology & Innovation

What's actually working versus what's just PowerPoint. AI, telehealth, wearables, and the platforms that matter.

Acquisition & Policy

NDAA provisions, contract vehicles, SBIR pathways, and navigating the complexity of federal procurement.
