About Mission Meets Tech

The people behind the intelligence

Mary Womack

Founder, Mission Meets Tech

Mary Womack is a federal health IT strategist with deep expertise in Defense Health Agency modernization, MHS GENESIS implementation, and the intersection of technology policy and military medicine.

Through Mission Meets Tech, she provides unfiltered analysis of the policies, technologies, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT—helping government contractors, defense stakeholders, and decision-makers cut through the noise to understand what actually matters.

Her newsletter reaches 1,191+ federal health IT professionals who want straight talk instead of vendor spin.

Sara Byrd

Federal Health IT Veteran & FedHealthIT100 Honoree

Sara Byrd brings decades of experience in federal health information technology, with a distinguished career spanning military health systems, VA modernization, and defense health policy.

As a FedHealthIT100 honoree, Sara is recognized as one of the most influential voices in federal health IT. She co-hosts the Fed UP podcast, bringing her insider perspective to discussions about what's really happening in military medicine.
