Why I Do This

I started Mission Meets Tech because I was fed up with the gap between what gets announced and what's actually happening in federal health IT.

The warfighter deserves better. The beneficiary deserves better. And the contractors and government leaders trying to make a difference deserve intelligence they can actually use.

I'm not here to repeat talking points or play it safe. I'm here to tell you what's really going on—the leadership moves, the contract dynamics, the policy shifts, and the technology bets that will determine who wins and who gets left behind.

"Does this save lives or enhance readiness?" — The question I come back to every time

The Podcast

Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality is the podcast I co-host with Sara Byrd. Together we bring different perspectives to federal health IT—I focus on strategic vision, policy, and enabling technology; Sara brings deep expertise in enterprise modernization and execution strategy.

The podcast extends the newsletter's mission into long-form conversation. Same commitment to substance over spin. Same focus on what actually matters for the warfighter and the beneficiary.
