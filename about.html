About

The person behind Mission Meets Tech

Mary Womack

Federal Civilian Account Lead, Rocket Data

I bridge health strategy and government/digital health, helping leaders navigate complexity with practical, mission-focused intelligence.

My expertise spans the Defense Health Agency (DHA), Veterans Affairs (VA), Military Health System (MHS) modernization, data governance, AI implementation, and federal acquisition processes. I'm fluent in the specialized terminology of this space: MHS GENESIS, TRICARE, MTFs, H2F programs, and the DARPA initiatives shaping the future of military medicine.

Before joining Rocket Data, I built my career at the intersection of federal health IT and commercial innovation, always focused on the same question: Does this save lives or enhance readiness?

Through Mission Meets Tech, I translate complex federal health IT policy into actionable intelligence for government contractors, defense stakeholders, and decision-makers. My audience consists primarily of senior-level decision-makers (59.4% CXO/director level) concentrated in the DC-Baltimore area.
