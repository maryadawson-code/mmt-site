Mary Womack

Publisher, Mission Meets Tech | Co-Host, Fed UP Podcast

2,000+ subscribers 500K+ monthly impressions 59% CXO/Director audience

Mary brings deep expertise in Defense Health Agency operations, VA health systems, MHS GENESIS implementation, and the intersection of federal health policy and emerging technology.

Her career spans the full spectrum of federal health IT—from hands-on program execution to strategic advisory work. She's built her reputation on translating complex policy into actionable intelligence, always anchored by one question: "Does this save lives or enhance readiness?"

Through Mission Meets Tech, Mary delivers the unfiltered analysis that federal health IT decision-makers need but rarely get from official channels. Her work focuses on leadership changes, contract intelligence, policy implications, and the technology trends reshaping military medicine.

Our Mission

We created Mission Meets Tech because we were fed up with the gap between what's announced and what's actually happening in federal health IT.

The warfighter deserves better. The beneficiary deserves better. And the contractors and government leaders trying to make a difference deserve intelligence they can actually use.

We're not here to repeat talking points or play it safe. We're here to tell you what's really going on—the leadership moves, the contract dynamics, the policy shifts, and the technology bets that will determine who wins and who gets left behind.
