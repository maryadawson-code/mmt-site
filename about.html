About

Hi, I'm Mary.

I help bridge the gap between health strategy and real-world impact, working at the intersection of government, digital health, and innovation.

My career has taken me from federal initiatives to industry partnerships, with one constant thread: a passion for advancing healthcare solutions that truly improve lives.

What I Bring to the Table

Health Strategy & Policy: Translating government goals into actionable roadmaps for better patient outcomes.

Digital Health Innovation: Accelerating adoption of AI, data, and emerging tech in healthcare delivery.

Partnerships & Alliances: Forging cross-sector collaborations that transform ideas into enterprise solutions.

Execution & Impact: Leading initiatives that de-risk adoption, streamline delivery, and generate measurable ROI.

My Sweet Spot

I help leaders navigate complexity, aligning strategy, policy, and technology to scale solutions that last.

At the end of the day, I believe innovation is only meaningful if it is scalable, sustainable, and saves lives.

Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality

I co-host the Fed UP podcast with Sara Byrd. Together, we dive deep into the policy fights, technology bets, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT and defense innovation.

Why "Fed UP"? Because we're fed up with the talking points. Fed up with vendors who pitch innovation theater while the warfighter waits. And frankly, fed up with watching the same patterns repeat while the threat environment accelerates.

But we're also fired up about the leaders who get it, the technologies that actually work, and the mission that keeps us coming back to this space.
