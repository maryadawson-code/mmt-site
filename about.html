Meet Mission Meets Tech

Watch our introduction to what we do and why it matters.

Mary Womack

Founder, Mission Meets Tech

Federal health IT strategist translating complex policy into actionable intelligence. I've spent my career at the intersection of federal health IT, defense contracting, and technology transformation—working on some of the most complex healthcare modernization programs in government.

Mission Meets Tech started as a way to share what I was learning with the broader community. It grew into something more: a platform for unfiltered analysis of what's actually happening in defense health IT, not the sanitized version you get in press releases.

My newsletter reaches defense health IT professionals across the DC-Baltimore corridor—CXOs, directors, and capture managers who need intelligence they can act on, not talking points they've already heard.

I also co-host Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality with Sara Byrd, where we dive deep into the policy fights and technology bets shaping federal health innovation.

One question drives everything I do: Does this save lives or enhance readiness?

Sara Byrd

Federal Health IT Veteran & FedHealthIT100 Honoree

Co-Founder of FedTechCares, a nonprofit promoting STEM and entrepreneurship for students and veterans. Deep expertise in defense acquisition and health IT spanning decades of federal service and private sector leadership.

Sara brings an operator's perspective to the Fed UP podcast—someone who's been in the rooms where decisions get made and understands the gap between policy intent and operational reality.

Her recognition as a FedHealthIT100 honoree reflects her sustained impact on federal health technology transformation, from large-scale EHR implementations to innovative telehealth deployments.

Her perspective: Policy without execution is just paper. Technology without adoption is just expense.
