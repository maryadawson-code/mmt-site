Mary Womack

Founder, Mission Meets Tech

I bridge health strategy and government/digital health, helping leaders navigate complexity with practical, mission-focused intelligence.

With deep expertise in defense health acquisition, data governance, and federal health IT modernization, I translate policy complexity into actionable insights for contractors and decision-makers navigating the Defense Health Agency, Veterans Affairs, and Military Health System landscape.

The question I keep coming back to: Does this save lives or enhance readiness? That's the filter for everything I cover.

What I Do

Mission Meets Tech Newsletter — My weekly analysis reaches over 1,100 subscribers, with 59% at the CXO or director level. No vendor pitches, no talking points—just unfiltered intelligence on the policy fights, technology bets, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT.

Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality — I co-host this podcast with Sara Byrd, diving deep into the stories behind the headlines. We're fed up with innovation theater and fired up about the leaders and technologies that actually work.

My Approach

I believe in giving people the intelligence they need to make better decisions—without the spin. The federal health IT space is complicated enough without adding vendor marketing or bureaucratic doublespeak.

Every newsletter issue, every podcast episode, every analysis comes back to the same question: Does this help the mission? If it doesn't save lives or enhance readiness, I'm not interested.

I'm also a big believer in the power of community. The best insights often come from the practitioners in the trenches—the contracting officers, the clinicians, the program managers who see what's really happening. That's why I try to build bridges between different parts of this ecosystem.
