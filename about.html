About Mission Meets Tech

Translating complex federal health IT policy into actionable intelligence since 2024.

Mary Womack

Federal Civilian Account Lead at Rocket Data

I've spent my career at the intersection of federal health IT, defense contracting, and technology transformation. After years at Leidos working on some of the most complex healthcare modernization programs in government, I joined Rocket Data to help bring commercial best practices to federal health challenges.

Mission Meets Tech started as a way to share what I was learning with the broader community. It grew into something more: a platform for unfiltered analysis of what's actually happening in defense health IT, not the sanitized version you get in press releases.

My newsletter reaches 1,191+ subscribers, with 59.4% at CXO or director level. These are the decision-makers who need intelligence they can act on, not talking points they've already heard.

I also co-host Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality with Sara Byrd, where we dive deep into the policy fights and technology bets shaping federal health innovation.

One question drives everything I do: Does this save lives or enhance readiness?
