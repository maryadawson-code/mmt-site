About Us

Fed up with the talking points. Fired up about what actually works. We're bringing clarity to federal health IT.

"Does this save lives or enhance readiness?"

One question drives everything we do. The Military Health System serves 9.5 million beneficiaries and supports the readiness of America's fighting force. We cut through the noise to deliver intelligence that actually matters — for contractors, government leaders, and anyone who cares about the warfighter.

Publisher & Co-Host

Mary Womack

Mission Meets Tech | Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality

Mary Womack is the publisher of Mission Meets Tech and co-host of the Fed UP podcast, delivering unfiltered intelligence on federal health IT transformation to defense contractors, government leaders, and industry decision-makers.

With deep expertise spanning the Defense Health Agency, Veterans Affairs, Military Health System modernization, data governance, and AI implementation, Mary translates complex policy into actionable intelligence. Her analysis has reached hundreds of thousands of impressions, with 59% of her newsletter subscribers at the CXO or director level.

Mary's career spans the intersection of healthcare technology and federal acquisition, giving her unique insight into what actually works — and what's just innovation theater.

Mary's work is anchored by one question: "Does this save lives or enhance readiness?"

Co-Host

Sara Byrd

Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality

Sara Byrd is the co-host of Fed UP and a recognized leader in federal health technology. As VP at Pantheon Data and co-founder of FedTechCares, Sara brings a sharp perspective on how technology can serve the mission — and where the system falls short.

Sara's background in defense health innovation and her passion for veteran care make her the perfect counterpoint in every Fed UP conversation. Together, Mary and Sara tackle the policy fights, technology bets, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT.
