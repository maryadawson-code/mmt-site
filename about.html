About Mission Meets Tech

Translating complex federal health IT policy into actionable intelligence since 2024.

Mary Womack

Founder, Mission Meets Tech

I've spent my career at the intersection of federal health IT, defense contracting, and technology transformation. I've worked on some of the most complex healthcare modernization programs in government, giving me an inside view of how the Military Health System really works.

Mission Meets Tech started as a way to share what I was learning with the broader community. It grew into something more: a platform for unfiltered analysis of what's actually happening in defense health IT, not the sanitized version you get in press releases.

I also co-host Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality with Sara Byrd, where we dive deep into the policy fights and technology bets shaping federal health innovation.

One question drives everything I do: Does this save lives or enhance readiness?

Meet My Co-Host

Sara Byrd

Federal Health IT Veteran & FedHealthIT100 Honoree

Sara brings deep federal health IT expertise from years of experience in defense acquisition, business development, and health technology leadership. She's a FedHealthIT100 Honoree who understands both the policy landscape and the operational realities of delivering technology in government.

As Co-Founder of FedTechCares, Sara is committed to promoting STEM education and entrepreneurship opportunities for students and veterans—bringing the next generation into federal technology.

Together, we cut through the noise on Fed UP to give you the unfiltered truth about what's working, what's not, and what you need to know.
