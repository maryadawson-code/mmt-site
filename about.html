The person behind Mission Meets Tech

MW

Mary Womack

Founder, Mission Meets Tech

I bridge health strategy with government and digital health expertise, helping leaders navigate the complexity of federal health IT, defense contracting, and military medicine transformation.

My core filter for every piece of content I produce: Does this save lives or enhance readiness? If it doesn't pass that test, it doesn't make the cut.

Through Mission Meets Tech, I provide unfiltered analysis for government contractors and decision-makers navigating the Defense Health Agency, Veterans Affairs, and military health system modernization. No talking points. No vendor spin. Just the intelligence you need to make informed decisions.

I co-host the Fed UP: Where Mission Meets Reality podcast with Sara Byrd, where we dive deep into the policy fights, technology bets, and leadership decisions shaping federal health IT and defense innovation.
